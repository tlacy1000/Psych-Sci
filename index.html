About conference

Welcome to the Psychedelic Science Summit

Together, we’ll discover the latest research into psychedelic therapy for the treatment of posttraumatic stress disorder (PTSD), addiction, depression, anxiety, and more. We’ll also learn from the experts about the risks and benefits of psychedelics such as MDMA, LSD, psilocybin, ketamine, and ayahuasca for mental health and personal growth, and the powerful role this work is playing in policy, law, and culture.
